Our Story


Le Bernardin Hotels started in 2004 was founded and managed by Mr. S. Raju who rewritten the service industry with homely care and rich heritage in the city of Coimbatore. Commitment to quality and transparency to the customers by providing homely food is the core values you can feel in our restaurants. These values continue to drive the inspiration at hotel. We listen to our quests closely and constantly and are always upgrading our services, facilities and infrastructure to keep pace with their changing needs.

History Of Le Bernardin

Our Journey

Here we wish to share a fruitful history of Le Bernardin Hotels. Our humble starting was the result of innovative idea of Mr. Raju who was in charge of Le Bernardin lodge situated at 4th street, Gandhipuram, Coimbatore. As all inmates of the lodge were looking for some place to have a homely food. Our founder took the initiative to provide homely food to the inmates of Le Bernardin lodge which gradually developed in to Le Bernardin Hotels or commonly known as Raju mess among the Non Vegetarian food lovers.
